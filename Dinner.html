Welcome to the Dinner Page!
choose one of this options as your Dinner

Moroccan Beans

Moroccan spiced beans are a tasty and protein-rich dish made with tender beans, fragrant spices, onions, and garlic. Perfect as a vegetarian main served.

Moroccan Bisara

A traditional Moroccan soup made with fava beans, spices, and olive oil, served with bread, is a great option for a late dinner.

Moroccan Briwat

These small pastry triangles filled with meat, vegetables, or cheese, are a great option for a late-night snack before bed.

Moroccan Chakchuka

This dish consists of eggs poached in a tomato-based sauce, making it a filling and healthy option for a late dinner.

Moroccan Harira

This hearty soup is perfect for a late-night meal, as it is packed with protein and fiber and can keep you full through the night.

Moroccan Kabab

a dish made with grilled or roasted meat, often beef or lamb, that has been marinated in a blend of Moroccan spices and herbs.

Moroccan Kafta

This dish is a great option for a late dinner, as the protein from the meatballs and the vegetables make it a satisfying and filling meal.

Moroccan roasted chicken with rice

a deliciously spiced dish that combines tender chicken, aromatic spices, sweet raisins, and earthy almonds. Served over fluffy and lightly seasoned rice, it's a satisfying and flavorful meal.

Moroccan Salad

A fresh and light salad made with lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and herbs, dressed with lemon juice and olive oil, is a great option for a light dinner before sleeping.
