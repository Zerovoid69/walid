Welcome to the Candy Page!
choose one of this options as your home made sweet

Moroccan til Chikki

a traditional Moroccan candy made from roasted sesame seeds and honey.

Moroccan Corne de gazelle

A soft and flaky pastry that is filled with almond paste and dusted with powdered sugar.
It is typically shaped like a crescent moon.

Moroccan Fekkas

A crunchy and slightly sweet cookie that is studded with almonds and sesame seeds.
It is often eaten as a snack or served with tea.

Moroccan Ghoriba

A dense and chewy cookie made from ground almonds, sugar, and eggs. It comes in a variety of flavors, such as coconut, chocolate, and lemon.

Moroccan Ghriba

This hearty soup is perfect for a late-night meal, as it is packed with protein and fiber and can keep you full through the night.

Moroccan Kaab al azal

crescent-shaped pastry that is filled with almond paste and flavored with orange blossom water.
It is often served during Eid al-Fitr.

Moroccan Makroud

A small, round cookie made from semolina and honey. It is often flavored with cinnamon and can be served with tea or coffee.

Moroccan Makruta

A fried and diamond-shaped pastry that is filled with a date paste and flavored with orange blossom water.
It is often served during Ramadan or special occasions.

Moroccan Mhincha

A rolled pastry that is stuffed with a mixture of almonds, sugar, and cinnamon. It is often shaped like a snake and served during special occasions.
